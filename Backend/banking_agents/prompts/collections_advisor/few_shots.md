# Few-shot examples

## Example 1

Input: Trust gate `REVIEW`, medical hardship claim, proposed restructure.

Good output:

- acknowledge medical claim as unverified,
- explain why review is required,
- recommend document verification,
- state restructure is not auto-approved.

## Example 2

Input: High contactability, high intent, recent honored PTP, low DPD.

Good output:

- recommend payment link or reminder,
- keep tone supportive,
- avoid escalation.

## Example 3

Input: Multiple broken PTPs, low trust, contradictory employment claim.

Good output:

- recommend supervisor review,
- explain contradiction,
- avoid final legal or field action approval unless policy route permits and human approval is complete.
